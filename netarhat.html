Netarhat - Queen of Chotanagpur

Location

Netarhat is located in Latehar district of Jharkhand, around 156 km from Ranchi.

About & History

Netarhat is a beautiful hill station in Jharkhand, often called the "Queen of Chotanagpur." It is famous for its scenic beauty, cool climate, and breathtaking sunrise and sunset views. The place is surrounded by dense forests, hills, and peaceful natural landscapes. Netarhat became popular during the British period when it was developed as a summer retreat. The famous Netarhat Residential School was established here in 1954 and is one of the most prestigious schools in the state. One of the main attractions is Magnolia Sunset Point, where visitors gather in the evening to witness a spectacular sunset. Sunrise Point is equally beautiful and offers a peaceful morning experience with mist-covered hills and golden sunlight. The region is rich in tribal culture and natural biodiversity. Tourists visit Netarhat for trekking, photography, and relaxation. The cool weather throughout the year makes it an ideal destination for families and nature lovers. Today, Netarhat stands as one of the top tourist destinations in Jharkhand and attracts visitors who want to enjoy nature away from city noise and pollution.

Hundru Falls

Location

Located near Ranchi, Jharkhand, approximately 45 km from the city.

About & History

Hundru Falls is one of the highest waterfalls in Jharkhand, formed by the Subarnarekha River. It falls from a height of about 98 meters, creating a spectacular natural view. The waterfall is surrounded by dense forests, rocky landscapes, and peaceful scenery, making it one of the most popular tourist destinations in the state. Historically, the region around Hundru Falls has been part of the Chotanagpur Plateau, known for its rich tribal culture and natural beauty. The waterfall becomes especially attractive during the monsoon season when the water flow increases dramatically. Visitors can enjoy trekking down the steps to reach the base of the waterfall. The sound of flowing water, fresh air, and scenic environment provide a relaxing experience. Many tourists visit for photography, picnics, and nature exploration. Over the years, the Jharkhand tourism department has developed the area with better pathways and safety measures to make it more accessible. Today, Hundru Falls stands as a symbol of Jharkhand’s natural beauty and attracts thousands of visitors every year.

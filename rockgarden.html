Rock Garden, Ranchi

Location

Rock Garden is located in Ranchi, Jharkhand, near Kanke Dam and around 4 km from the city center.

About & History

Rock Garden in Ranchi is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Jharkhand. It was developed by the Jharkhand Tourism Department by carving natural rocks into beautiful sculptures and artistic structures. The garden is built using rocks from the nearby Gonda Hill, giving it a natural and eco-friendly design. The main attraction of Rock Garden is its stone sculptures, artificial waterfalls, and scenic viewpoints. From the top of the garden, visitors can enjoy a beautiful panoramic view of Kanke Dam and the surrounding greenery. The location is especially popular during evenings when the sunset creates a breathtaking view. Historically, the area was part of the Chotanagpur Plateau region, known for its rocky terrain and tribal heritage. The garden was developed to promote tourism and provide a recreational space for families and visitors. Over the years, it has become a favorite picnic spot for locals and tourists. Rock Garden is also a great place for photography, relaxation, and spending time in nature. The combination of artistic rock structures and natural beauty makes it a must-visit destination in Ranchi. Today, it stands as a symbol of creative architecture blended with nature in Jharkhand.
